METAL ROLLER BENDER

ABSRACT

To design and construct a bending machine that is used to bend steel bar into curve or other curvature shape.

INTRODUCTION

To built this machine many equipment or machine is used. By using all this equipment, process for making the project is faster and easier. We have also learned a proper method for operating all this machine and equipment. Choosing component material is very important because it will affect the overall cost of the machine and the product quality. With this consideration, We have designed this machine with the maximum quality and low in cost.

WORKING

The bar between the Three rollers will take on the shape of a cubic polynomial, which approximates a circular arc.The Three rollers are then rotated moving the bar along with them. For each new position, the portion of the bar between the three rollers takes on the shape of a cubic modified by the end conditions imposed by the adjacent section of the bar.When either end of the bar is reached, the force applied to the center roller is incrementally increased, the roller rotations reversed and as the rolling process proceeds, the bar shape becomes a better approximation to a circular arc, gradually, for the number of passes required to bring the arc of the bar to the desired radius.
